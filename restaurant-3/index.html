About Us

Chip's Fish & Chips opened in the year 2020. Remember what a crummy year that was? We somehow managed to make it through that year and have been serving our visitors with mouthwatering seafood and delicious chips ever since. We fly our saltwater fare in every day from around the world. We craft our chips from nothing but the best potatoes. The bar has a wide-selection of adult beverages and our bartenders are knowledgable and friendly. Our desserts are culinary delights, perfect for finishing off your meal. And, our table service can't be beat; we guarantee you'll leave with a smile on your face. We fill up fast. Reserve a table today!
